DC Mythcrafting: Flash

Jason "Jay" Garrick was a graduate student and physical chemist working on the Change Engine Project with his advisor and mentor, Professor Hughes. When the Change Engine became unstable and exploded the safety mechanisms dropped it in a pool of heavy water. Jay and fellow student, Edward Clariss were caught in the explosion and gouts of heavy water steam.

Jay gained superspeed and became the Flash. Major Arthur Williams was the military liason and security officer in charge of the Change Engine Project. Jay started a relationship with his daughter Joan Williams. After the Change Engine Project was shutdown, a gang of Fifth Columnists known as the Faultless Four kidnapped Major Williams to gain the secrets of the Change Engine from him.

Jay rescued Major Williams and captured the Faultless Four.
